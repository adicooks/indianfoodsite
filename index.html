About Our Restaurant

Tandoori Flames is an Indian cuisine that is known for its delicious and aromatic dishes. The restaurant is named after the traditional tandoor oven, which is used to cook many of their signature dishes. The tandoor oven creates a smoky, char-grilled flavor that is unique to Indian cuisine. Tandoori Flames offers a wide variety of dishes. In addition to traditional Indian dishes, the restaurant also offers fusion dishes that incorporate flavors from other cuisines. With its flavorful food and warm, welcoming atmosphere, Tandoori Flames is the perfect place to enjoy a delicious and authentic Indian meal.
